Drowsy Gorilla and Dave

Drowsy Gorilla and Dave are a rough and tumble team. Drowsy Gorilla tends to fall asleep during the missions and Dave the Emu always bails him out. Drowsy Gorilla wakes up just long enough to see that the job was completed and gains the praise of others. Dave the Emu is just happy to be on the team.
